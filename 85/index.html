About 85PhotoBooth

85-Photobooth rentals services the wedding, corporate event, bar/bat mitzvah, sweet sixteen, birthday and private event space with a customized photo booth studio. Professional studio lighting and cameras provide for exceedingly elegant and original capture superior to ordinary photobooths. On site projection and printing are both available.

This custom photobooth rental service is owned and operated by 85 Photo Productions Inc. Enrique Cubillo is the principal photographer and owner. Enrique's career spans over 20 years creating images for industry, public relations and advertising. His company has photographed hundreds of events. Backgrounds may be live or fully customizable. The set can be arranged to accommodate one to twenty guests and is fully automated beginning with the push of a button by the guest.
