Craig Fossella

Craig Fossella has spent his career identifying opportunities to create new environments in the health and beauty field. As a senior executive with a public laser company in the 1990’s, Craig successfully married medical services into a day spa, creating the first Medi-Spa.

Fossella followed that up by taking Bliss from a single location day spa in New York City to an international powerhouse brand. Bliss became perhaps the most well known spa brand in the world and was known as the leader of the spa boom because it brought spa services to the masses, due in large part to its friendly facilities and irreverent branding.

In 2003, he moved west to launch his own project, Tru in San Francisco. Tru became not only the talk of San Francisco, but garnered a tremendous amount of national attention. Many of the Tru’s concepts that were revolutionary for the time are now standard in the spa industry. Fossella is also the owner of Bare, an upscale waxing boutique in the Marina district of San Francisco.
