Safety


At Bisou, we take your safety very seriously. We are dedicated to the highest level of sanitation at the salon.

We use stainless steel bowls for our pedicures. Why is that significant? Because we know we can clean all the surfaces, unlike in a whirlpool bath where bacteria gets inside the jets.

We also use a surgical grade autoclave to sterilize our metal tools. Autoclaves use pressure to raise the boiling point of water to a temperature that kills bacteria, fungi, and salon-borne infections. This method is the highest level of sterilization and is usually used in medical applications.
