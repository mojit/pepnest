Bisou Nail Lounge is the first organic nail salon in San Francisco, Bay Area. The products we use are completely natural, organic and/or biodynamic. We believe that beauty and comfort today must be in a natural environment.

We are also a proud pioneer of gel nails in Northern California using Calgel and Bella Forma, as well as being known for our gel nail art and custom designs.
